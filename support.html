For Music Fans (Spotters): Create an account to discover shows, follow artists, and purchase tickets.

For Venues: Register your venue to host shows, manage bookings, and attract new audiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.

How do payments work?
All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

Can I cancel a ticket purchase?
Please contact the venue directly for their cancellation policy.
